Meet Ivan Born on November 20, 2020

Ivan is the most loving and kind gift we could have ever received. He fills our days with joy and laughter, and his curiosity knows no bounds. Ivan’s heart comes alive when he sings worship songs and praises God, a testament to his gentle spirit. One of his greatest fascinations is with clocks—especially majestic tower clocks—and he can spend hours marveling at their movements. His creative side shines when he plays with blocks and magnetic tiles, building worlds of wonder and imagination. Every photo in this collection reflects a precious moment in Ivan’s journey, capturing the essence of who he is and the love that surrounds him.

Ivan’s School Life
Ivan is thriving in Campbell Christian School, where he is currently enrolled in preschool. Each day is an adventure filled with learning and laughter. His love for music extends beyond the classroom—he eagerly attends music school, where he continues to nurture his passion.
Among all his wonderful teachers, Ivan’s heart is especially fond of Ms. Jennifer, his beloved teacher from Bible Sunday school, whose lessons and kindness have left a lasting impression on him.
